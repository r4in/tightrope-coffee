About Us

PASSION + COMMITMENT

Tightrope Coffee is located M. L. Quezon Avenue, F. Cabahug St.

The coffee house offers a variety of choices to the customers. Coffee and tea of all sorts will be offered. The choices of coffee will range from espresso to latte, long black coffee to duets, and different kinds of coffee filters.

For those who want something else to drink, hot chocolates, fresh watermelon and sparkling lemonade can be a refreshing alternative.

The breakfast menu features full and sumptuous meals such as the famous PB&E and the superb Tightrope breakfast. Lunch and dinner features include the best Bagnet- a crunchy thick cut pork belly served with seasoned vinegar, Arroz Con Pollo, Torikatsu and Chicken Parmesan. In addition, Tightrope Coffee features sandwiches, pastas, sides, and desserts.

The interior design of the building projects a relaxed atmosphere with a hint of lux and minimalism..

The emphasis will be on providing a comfortable environment for the customer where they will want to linger and return many times.
